If you have a pair of leather boots with a broken zipper or a tear in your favourite handbag, do not fret! Bring your items to Leslie's Luggage & Shoe Repair for repair services at competitive prices.

Whether you require sole replacements, strap repairs or one shoe made taller to accommodate you, we can be of assistance. We carry many parts and supplies from some of the leading manufacturers, including Vibram, TOPY, and Birkenstock, to name but a few.

We are located three blocks east from the Broadway & City Hall SkyTrain station in Vancouver. We are easily accessible by car, bus or SkyTrain.

To inquire about the availability of our services or to obtain an estimate, please give us a call today or email us at lesliesrepair@gmail.com along with a picture of your item with a brief description.
